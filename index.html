About Me

I’m currently a second-year Master’s student in Theoretical High-Energy Physics at the University of Amsterdam. I hold a Master’s degree in Physics from King’s College London and a Bachelor’s degree in Materials Science and Engineering from QMES, NPU.

Outside of physics, I’m into Japanese Sengoku history and football — I’ve supported Manchester United for years. In my spare time, I enjoy RTS games like Age of Empires IV, and I often unwind with light music. Xeuphoria is my favourite composer.

Research Interests

My research interests broadly concern the quantum aspects of de Sitter spacetime, with particular focus on path-integral formulations of quantum cosmology, the role of the observer, and dS holography proposals.

  • Quantum aspects of de Sitter
  • Path-integral quantum cosmology
  • Observer in cosmology
  • de Sitter holography
