I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Yau Mathematical Sciences Center at Tsinghua University. My research focuses on quantum and classical theories of gravity, conformal field theories, and their relation via the AdS/CFT correspondence. In particular, I'm interested in using (conformal) field theories to understand universal features of quantum gravity and holography. Some of the topics of my research include

  • non-AdS holography and 3d gravity
  • bootstrapping 2d conformal field theories
  • non-linear theories of interacting spin-2 fields

Currently I'm studying a class of solvable irrelevant deformations of 2d conformal field theories and their connection to 4d extremal Kerr black holes. You can read more about it here:

  • L. Apolo and W. Song, "Heating up holography for single-trace JTbar deformations" [1907.03745]
  • L. Apolo and W. Song, "Strings on warped AdS3 via TJbar deformations" [1806.10127]
