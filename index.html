Profile Summary

I am a second-year Master’s student at the University of Amsterdam (UvA), specializing in the foundational questions of **Quantum Gravity and Cosmology**. My work leverages advanced theoretical methods to investigate the properties of de Sitter spacetime.

My academic path includes prior Master's study at King's College London, following an undergraduate degree in Material Science and Engineering.

Research Interests

My core focus is on the quantum description of the early universe, including:

  • The **No-Boundary Wave Function** and theories of cosmic initial conditions.
  • Application of **Path-Integral** methods to quantum cosmological models.
  • Theoretical connections between gravity and field theory, such as **dS Holography**.
