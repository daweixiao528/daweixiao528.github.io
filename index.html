About Me

关于我
MSc @ UvA

I’m currently a second-year Master’s student in Theoretical High-Energy Physics at the University of Amsterdam. I hold a Master’s degree in Physics from King’s College London and a Bachelor’s degree in Materials Science and Engineering from QMES, NPU.

Outside of physics, I have an interest in Japanese Sengoku history. I’m also a passionate football fan and have supported Manchester United for years. In my free time, I enjoy playing RTS games like Age of Empires IV, and I often unwind by listening to light music — Xeuphoria is my favourite composer.

Research Interests

研究方向
de Sitter & quantum cosmology

My research interests broadly concern the quantum aspects of de Sitter spacetime, with particular focus on the path-integral formulations of quantum cosmology, the role of the observer, and dS holography proposals.

  • Quantum aspects of de Sitter spacetime
  • Path-integral approaches to quantum cosmology
  • Role of the observer in cosmology
  • de Sitter holography proposals
