I’m a second-year Master’s student at UvA, thinking about quantum aspects of de Sitter spacetime and related questions in quantum cosmology.

Previously, I completed an MSc in Physics at King’s College London and a BEng in Materials Science and Engineering at QMES (NPU / QMUL joint program).

Outside physics

When I’m not working on cosmology, I read about Japanese Sengoku history, follow football (I’ve supported Manchester United for years), and play RTS games like Age of Empires IV. For music, I often loop light / chill tracks — Xeuphoria is my favourite composer.

Research & interests

My research interests broadly concern the quantum aspects of de Sitter spacetime, with particular focus on:

  • Path-integral formulations of quantum cosmology
  • The role of the observer in cosmology
  • de Sitter holography proposals

At the moment, I’m especially curious about how to formulate “who is observing what” in a cosmological setting, and what path integrals over geometries should really mean in de Sitter space.
