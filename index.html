What I'm doing now

Amsterdam · 2024–2026

I’m a second-year MSc student in the theoretical physics track at UvA. Most of my time goes into understanding how to make sense of quantum fields and gravity in de Sitter spacetime.

On the side, I keep revisiting QFT basics, play around with thought experiments about observers in cosmology, and slowly expand a set of notes that may or may not ever become lecture-style material.

What I'm thinking about

mostly de Sitter

Broadly speaking, I’m interested in the quantum aspects of de Sitter spacetime. More concretely, I think about:

– Path-integral formulations of quantum cosmology
– The role of the observer in cosmology and how we should encode “who is observing what”
– de Sitter holography proposals and what they might be telling us
