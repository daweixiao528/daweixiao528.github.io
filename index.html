About Me

I am a second-year Master’s student specializing in **Theoretical High-Energy Physics** at the University of Amsterdam (UvA). My academic path includes an MSc in Physics from King's College London and a BEng in Materials Science and Engineering from NPU/QMUL.

My work focuses on the foundational questions of quantum gravity, specifically in the cosmological context of de Sitter spacetime.

Research Interests

My interests center on the quantum formulation of cosmology, including:

  • No-Boundary Wave Function and its implications for cosmic initial conditions.
  • Path-Integral Formulations of quantum cosmology.
  • dS Holography Proposals (e.g., dS/CFT correspondence).
