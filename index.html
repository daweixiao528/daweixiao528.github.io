I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Yau Mathematical Sciences Center at Tsinghua University. My research focuses on quantum and classical theories of gravity, conformal field theories, and their relation via the AdS/CFT correspondence.



Research

I'm interested in using (conformal) field theories to understand universal features of quantum gravity and holography. Some of the topics of my research include

  • non-AdS holography
  • bootstrapping 2d (conformal) field theories
  • non-linear theories of interacting spin-2 fields

Currently I'm studying a class of solvable irrelevant deformations of 2d conformal field theories and their connection to 4d extremal Kerr black holes. You can read more about it here and here.
