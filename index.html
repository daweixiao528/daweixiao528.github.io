About Me

I am a second-year Master’s student specializing in **Theoretical High-Energy Physics** at the University of Amsterdam (UvA). My academic background includes an MSc in Physics from King's College London and a BEng in Materials Science and Engineering from the NPU/QMUL Joint Program (QMES).

My core academic pursuit lies in unraveling the foundational questions at the intersection of quantum gravity and cosmology, seeking a deeper understanding of the quantum nature of spacetime.

Research Interests

My research is primarily centered on the **quantum aspects of de Sitter spacetime**. Key areas of investigation include:

  • **No-Boundary Wave Function**
  • **Path-Integral Formulations of Quantum Cosmology**
  • **dS Holography Proposals** (e.g., dS/CFT correspondence)
