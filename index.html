About Me

I’m currently a second-year Master’s student in **Theoretical High-Energy Physics** at the University of Amsterdam. I hold a Master’s degree in Physics from King’s College London and a Bachelor’s degree in Materials Science and Engineering from QMES, NPU.

Outside of physics, I have an interest in Japanese Sengoku history. I’m also a passionate football fan and have supported Manchester United for years. In my free time, I enjoy playing RTS games like *Age of Empires IV*, and I often unwind by listening to light music—Xeuphoria is my favorite composer.

Research Interests

My research interests broadly concern the quantum aspects of de Sitter spacetime, with particular focus on the no-boundary wave function, path-integral formulations of quantum cosmology, and dS holography proposals.
